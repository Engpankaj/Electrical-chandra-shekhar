About Me

Chandra Shekhar

My name is Chandra Shekhar, and I am a professional electrician with over 10 years of hands-on experience in residential and commercial electrical work. I specialize in house wiring, panel installation, switchboard fitting, fan installation, LED lighting setup, AC wiring, and complete electrical maintenance services.

I am committed to delivering safe, high-quality, and timely work on every project. Customer satisfaction is my top priority, and I ensure that every task is completed with precision and responsibility.

If you are looking for reliable electrical installation, repair, or maintenance services, I am always ready to assist you.
